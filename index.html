List Inclusion Criteria
  • Lists must be based exclusively on Geometry Dash or very similar GDPS.
  • Lists should not include any content from any other game.
  • Lists should contain at least 1 level.
  • Lists must exist at present time.
  • Lists must rank levels in some order.
Duplicates are removed across levels per rules; Discord-only and backups keep all entries
